About BLINK

Who we are? .. What we do?

BLINK is a limited liability company (LLC) founded in late 2013, after winning the gold prize in "StartUp-Egypt" competition organized by Injaz-Egypt and gaining our first seed fund.

We develop Augmented Reality applications. It's a cutting-edge technology that merges the real world with computer generated content which enables a whole new possibilities in every aspect of our life.

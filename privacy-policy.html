1. INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

USERS

This Privacy Policy sets forth the terms and conditions under which we will collect and process your personal data when we provide the Service.

If you are a user, whenever you open a layer when using our Service, you will be connected automatically with the server of the Third Party Content provider. Our system transmits an anonymous hash, which cannot be traced back to the device used to activate the Service. Alternatively, you may access the Service through a user account you have set up with a publisher or another third party. We will not store any personal data that relate to your user account with that third party, and we advise you to read the privacy policy applied by such third party, as these terms will also apply to you. Please note that these third parties may apply their own policies with respect to cookies.

We allow you to login through social networks that you have linked to, provided that you have fully complied with their requirements on creating a user account, and that you refer to such networks as part of your profile.

All Third Party Content which you can access through the Service is provided to you by those third parties. By accessing Third Party Content, you grant us permission to collect, use, process and store any of your personal data that may be contained therein.

Our servers will aggregate, store and process data in respect of Reference Images. You grant us permission to collect, use, process and store any of your personal data that are contained therein.

By exchanging, providing and/or uploading User Generated Content, you grant us permission to use, store and process any of your personal data that are contained therein. You should be fully aware that any personal data of third parties that are contained in your User Generated Content are subject to data protection and other legislation and regulations and you are fully responsible for compliance with these laws and regulations. We require that you exercise due care when exchanging personal data of others as part of your User Generated Content.

Only you determine the purposes for which you collect, use and disclose content, including your User Generated Content, through the App or the Reality Browser; you are the controller with regard to these data.

LOG DATA AND LOCATION DATA

Our servers automatically record:

information created by your use of and contribution to the Service (“Log Data”); and

images the Developer supplies to BLINK in order to enable the extraction by users of features for recognition through the Service.

Log Data may include information such as your phone ID, browser type, click paths and search terms. Interactions between users and publishers may also be included in Log Data. We will aggregate and anonymize the Log Data, add up all individual events and only use the aggregate data for statistical purposes. We remove the Log Data every two months.

We will collect, use and store location data for which you gave us your consent, including but not limited to your GPS-location, which were provided by you or which we receive from GPS location service providers but only for as long as this is necessary for delivering our Service and/or to the extent required or permitted by applicable law.
